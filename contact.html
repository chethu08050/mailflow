Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MailFlow Pro

How quickly can I get started with MailFlow Pro?

You can create an account and start sending emails within minutes. Our intuitive setup wizard guides you through the process, and our team is available to assist if you need any help.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team with details about your organization to discuss eligibility.

What integrations do you support?

MailFlow Pro integrates with popular CRM platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, and Zoho, as well as e-commerce platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce. We also offer API access for custom integrations.

How do you ensure email deliverability?

We maintain high deliverability rates through authenticated sending domains, proper list hygiene practices, and continuous monitoring of sender reputation. Our infrastructure is designed to meet ISP guidelines and requirements.
